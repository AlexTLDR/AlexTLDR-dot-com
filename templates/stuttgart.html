a place for gophers to network

In 2023, motivated by my passion for Go, I joined several Go groups on meetup.com. However, none were in my area. Being based in the Stuttgart region, I looked for a local group but couldn't find one. So, in December 2023, I founded the Stuttgart Gophers, and we held our first online event in January 2024. Since then, the community has grown, and I’ve received help from one of the members in managing the group. The group's goal is to provide a place for gophers to learn, network, and have fun.
